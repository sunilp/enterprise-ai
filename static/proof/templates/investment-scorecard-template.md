## Scoring Criteria

Score each criterion on a 1-5 scale using the definitions below.

| Criterion | What it measures | 1 (Lowest) | 3 (Mid) | 5 (Highest) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Strategic Alignment** | Degree to which the use case advances stated business priorities | No clear link to strategy | Supports a secondary priority | Directly advances a top-3 strategic objective |
| **Data Readiness** | Quality, availability, and governance of data needed | Data does not exist or is inaccessible | Data exists but has gaps or quality issues | Clean, accessible, governed data ready now |
| **Process Readiness** | Maturity of the business process the AI will operate within | Process undefined or highly variable | Process documented but inconsistently followed | Stable, documented, and actively managed process |
| **Expected Value** | Magnitude of financial or strategic benefit | Marginal or unmeasurable | Moderate benefit with plausible case | Substantial, quantifiable benefit |
| **Governance Complexity** | Risk, compliance burden, and oversight requirements (lower complexity = higher score) | High-risk, heavily regulated, unresolved ethical concerns | Moderate risk with some regulatory exposure | Low risk, minimal regulatory exposure, clear ownership |
| **Time to Value** | How quickly value can be realised after go decision | Longer than 18 months | 6-18 months | Under 6 months |

## Scoring Guide

| Total Score | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| 25-30 | **Invest Now** -- Strong candidate. Prioritise for next programme increment. |
| 18-24 | **Invest with Conditions** -- Address the lowest-scoring dimension before full commitment. |
| 12-17 | **Defer** -- Address readiness gaps and re-score in the next cycle. |
| Below 12 | **Do Not Proceed** -- Insufficient strategic fit or readiness. Revisit only if context materially changes. |
